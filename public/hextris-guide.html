The Ultimate Guide to Hextris (Free 2025 Edition)

Hextris is a fast-paced puzzle game inspired by Tetris. In this 2025 free unblocked version, your goal is to stop blocks from leaving the inside of the outer gray hexagon.

How to Play

Use the left and right arrow keys to rotate the hexagon. Match 3 or more blocks of the same color to clear them. If the blocks stack outside the outer hexagon, the game ends.

Winning Strategies

  • Keep it flat: Try to keep the stacks on each side of the hexagon relatively even. A tall stack on one side is dangerous.
  • Look ahead: Like Tetris, anticipation is key. Watch the incoming colors.
  • Speed matters: As time goes on, the blocks fall faster. Stay calm and tap quickly.

Why Play Hextris?

It improves cognitive speed and pattern recognition. Plus, it's a perfect no-download game for quick breaks.
