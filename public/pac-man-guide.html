Pac-Man: Retro Arcade Guide

Waka Waka! The original arcade king is back. Enjoy this free 2025 version of Pac-Man without any quarters needed.

Ghost Personalities

  • Blinky (Red): Chases you directly.
  • Pinky (Pink): Tries to ambush you from the front.
  • Inky (Blue): Unpredictable, uses Blinky's position.
  • Clyde (Orange): Wanders off when he gets too close.

High Score Tips

Clear the bottom corners first as they are dangerous traps. Use the tunnels to lose ghosts—you move faster in tunnels than they do.
