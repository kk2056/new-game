Tower Stack: Build to the Sky

Tower Stack is a precision arcade game. In this free 2025 guide, learn how to build the highest tower without toppling over.

Gameplay

Tap screen or click mouse to drop the moving block. The goal is to place it perfectly on top of the previous block. Any overhang is sliced off, making the next block smaller.

Perfect Drops

If you align the block perfectly, you get a "Perfect" score and the block size might grow slightly. Chaining perfect drops is the key to a high score in this html5 instant play classic.

Focus is Key

The block moves faster as you go higher. Rhythm is more important than visual reaction. Feel the beat of the movement.
